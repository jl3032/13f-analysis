## 13F Reference Value Guide

| 13F Value | Meaning | Who |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| **HIGH** | 持仓稳定，每个变动都有意义，值得跟踪 | 段永平、巴菲特、Ackman、Klarman、Burry |

## Manager Profiles (Brief)

### 段永平 (Duan Yongping) — Himalaya Capital
- **背景:** 步步高、OPPO、vivo 创始人，中国最成功企业家转型投资人
- **Famous trades:** Apple, NetEase early bet ($1/share), Google/Alphabet
- **Style:** Ultra-concentrated (9 positions), Graham-Buffett value investing
- **Key quotes:** "Buy great businesses at reasonable prices" / "买的时候就想好拿10年"
- **13F note:** Very few positions, high conviction, any change is significant. 2006年带黄峥参加巴菲特午餐。

### Warren Buffett — Berkshire Hathaway
- **背景:** 股神，奥马哈先知，世界最成功投资人
- **Famous trades:** Coca-Cola (1988), Apple (2016), Bank of America
- **Style:** Buy and hold forever, "our favorite holding period is forever"
- **13F note:** Very stable portfolio, any new position is major news. 注意13F只显示股票，不含保险子公司的完全控股企业。

### Li Lu 李录 — Himalaya Capital Partners
- **背景:** 芒格说"如果李录生在美国，他会是下一个巴菲特"。管理芒格家族资金。
- **Style:** 极致集中，深度研究，中美两地投资
- **13F note:** 仓位很少，每个都是深思熟虑。常和段永平有交叉持仓。

### Michael Burry 大空头 — Scion Asset Management
- **背景:** 《大空头》主角，做空次贷一战成名
- **Famous trades:** 2008次贷空头, GameStop, 做空TSLA/ARKK
- **Style:** Contrarian deep value, 经常逆势操作
- **13F note:** 极度高换手！每个季度大幅变动，但每次新建仓都值得关注（他通常做过很深的研究）。

### Bill Ackman — Pershing Square
- **Famous trades:** Chipotle turnaround, Herbalife short (loss), Netflix, Universal Music
- **Style:** Concentrated activist, 8-12 positions
- **13F note:** Very few positions, each one matters, public about theses

### Cathie Wood 木头姐 — ARK Invest
- **Famous trades:** Tesla early conviction, Coinbase, genomics
- **Style:** Disruptive innovation, high growth, high conviction but high turnover
- **13F note:** ⚠️ 13F 参考价值低。ARK 每天公布持仓变动 (ark-funds.com)，比季度13F更有用。

### Stanley Druckenmiller — Duquesne
- **Famous trades:** Soros-era pound short (1992), 2020 tech plays
- **Style:** Macro + concentrated equity bets, willing to swing large
- **13F note:** Portfolio can change dramatically quarter to quarter. 关注他的新建仓方向。

### Seth Klarman — Baupost
- **Famous trades:** Distressed debt, contrarian equity plays
- **Style:** Deep value, Margin of Safety author, very private
- **13F note:** Positions can be revealing since Klarman rarely discusses publicly. 他是最神秘的大佬之一。

### David Einhorn — Greenlight Capital
- **Famous trades:** Lehman Brothers short (2008), Allied Capital, gold
- **Style:** Deep value + short selling, detailed research
- **13F note:** Concentrated, new positions often accompanied by public theses

### Tom Gayner — Markel
- **Style:** Long-term quality businesses, insurance float investing, patient
- **13F note:** Very stable, changes are deliberate and meaningful. 类似迷你版巴菲特。

### Mohnish Pabrai 帕布莱 — Pabrai Investment Funds
- **背景:** 印度裔价值投资人，巴菲特/芒格忠实信徒，写过《The Dhandho Investor》
- **Style:** 极致集中 (3-5 positions), 深度价值
- **13F note:** 仓位极少，每个变动都是重大信号。经常和段永平有类似审美。

